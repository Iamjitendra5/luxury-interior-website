About

Designing homes that feel composed, tactile, and personal.

Studio

We balance architectural clarity with soft, human detail.

VC Designs is a luxury interior design practice focused on modern residential spaces. Our work is quiet but never plain: precise layouts, expressive materials, tailored furniture, and lighting that changes the feeling of a room through the day.

We collaborate with homeowners, architects, craftsmen, and vendors to deliver interiors that are beautiful, functional, and built to age gracefully.

Refined Simplicity

Clean composition, restrained palettes, and thoughtful contrast create spaces that feel luxurious without excess.

Material Honesty

Stone, wood, metal, glass, linen, leather, and handmade finishes are chosen for beauty, durability, and touch.

Personal Living

Every decision begins with the client’s routines, rituals, collections, and expectations for everyday comfort.
